## FAQ for Exchanges

### Is ESTV audited?
Security audit is scheduled for Q1 2026 with CertiK/Hacken. Report will be published in `/audits` directory.

### What is the token utility?
ESTV is a utility token for the ESTV esports media platform - used for rewards, payments, governance, and staking within the ecosystem.

### Is there a vesting schedule?
Yes. Detailed vesting information available in [TOKENOMICS.md](./TOKENOMICS.md).

### What networks are supported?
Currently Solana Mainnet only. Cross-chain bridge is under consideration for future.

### Where can we verify the team?
Team profiles are linked to LinkedIn and can be verified at https://estvlabs.com/team
