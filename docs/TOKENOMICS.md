## Treasury Management

### Custody
- **Wallet**: Squads Protocol Multisig (Foundation-controlled)
- **Migration**: Completed (Personal → Squads Foundation)
- **Team Distribution**: Pending — will follow vesting schedule per category
- **Security**: Multi-signature approval required for all transfers

### Treasury Policy
- Maximum supply is fixed at 1B tokens
- Supply adjustments (burn/buyback) possible for operational purposes
- All actions governed by policy, compliance, and regulations
- No actions intended to influence market price

### Burn Mechanism
- Platform may allocate portion of fees to treasury
- Burn decisions based on internal policy and compliance
- Parameters are discretionary and may change
